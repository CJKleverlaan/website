  <p class=MsoNormal style='margin-bottom:0cm;line-height:normal'><span
  lang=EN-US style='font-family:"Calibri",sans-serif'>The HELIUS study is a
  prospective cohort study, including six ethnic groups (including the Dutch as
  a reference) living in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. The general objective of
  the HELIUS study is to study the causes of (the unequal burden of) diseases
  across these ethnic groups, with emphasis on three disease categories:
  cardiovascular diseases, mental health and infectious diseases.</span></p>
