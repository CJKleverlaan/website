ORANGEHEALTH-PPS “Innovation Booster” projects at Dutch Dental Science Days

Nov 20, 2025

On Thursday, March 19, and Friday, March 20, 2026, the annual Dutch Dental Science Days will take place at Hotel De Werelt in Lunteren. On Friday, March 20, the afternoon program will be hosted by the ORANGEHealth-PPS initiative. During this session, the newly awarded projects from the Innovation Booster round will present themselves. Everyone is warmly invited to join! Registration is available via: https://dds-days-2026.webnode.nl/
